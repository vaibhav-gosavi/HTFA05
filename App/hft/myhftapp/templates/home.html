About PS1 Solution by Coders Dungeon

Coders Dungeon presents a solution for PS1 using advanced machine learning techniques.

We employ Hashing techniques to anonymize personal details of users, ensuring privacy and data security.

K Means Clustering is utilized to organize and cluster the datasets, providing valuable insights into user behavior and preferences.

To assess the accuracy of each dataset, we leverage RandomForest, a powerful algorithm for classification and regression tasks.
